The album is a result of 19 "improvisations",
or on-the-spot compositions,
some of the pieces utilizing early music concepts
and some motivic development in a more general sense.
Thus the overall sound ranges from early music pastiches
to soundtrack-like music, or fantasias if you will.
Let's see where the wind takes me next.
This album was crafted by
engraving the musical motifs in LilyPond
and producing the music with Csound.
I will continue posting individual blog posts for each piece in:
[Wherever The Wind Takes Me (Category Page)](/categories/whereverthewindtakesme).
