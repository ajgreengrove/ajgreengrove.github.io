Gene Boggs - Eponymous (Featuring A J Greengrove) Album Release

A J Greengrove

Published: April 23, 2025

“Eponymous:” an almost unlikely combination of electronica and mellow guitars.

As I tend to do with albums, I (re-)compiled a youtube playlist of the song audios:

Minimal structures and textures meet the guitar that phrases longer melodic arcs. Delicate synths and recorded instruments meet plucked nylon strings. Hope you enjoy. Gene Boggs is Epistemologist-at-large. The featuring artist A J Greengrove is a musician in the early music sense: one who plays, improvises, composes both on-the-spot and with longer processes.
